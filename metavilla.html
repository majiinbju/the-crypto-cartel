hola familia, welcome to the crypto cartel

The Crypto Cartel is a network of 8,000 high-profile international cartel members who have turned into new leaves on the blockchain. The cryptocurrency wave of 2021 has pushed the bad guys to become the good guys. Once dangerous criminal masterminds, these goodfellas have given up their old ways of dealing in narcotics to strictly deal only in crypto henceforth. They have chosen to leave the evil world behind for their newfound love of the blockchain, where they plan to form a new cartel. This Cartel is unlike any other, with crypto dealers making their bones by doing good in both the real world and the metaverse through their cartel network. Long gone are the days of a singular kingpin and fighting over territory. The Crypto Cartel collection is a unique set of digital collectibles, stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS.
