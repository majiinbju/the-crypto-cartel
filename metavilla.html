welcome to the crypto cartel

The Crypto Cartel is a network of 8,000 high-profile international cartel members who have turned into new leaves on the blockchain. The cryptocurrency wave of 2021 has pushed the bad guys to become the good guys. Once dangerous criminal masterminds, these goodfellas have given up their old ways of dealing in narcotics to strictly deal only in crypto henceforth. They have chosen to leave the evil world behind for their newfound love of the blockchain, where they plan to form a new cartel. This Cartel is unlike any other, with crypto dealers making their bones by doing good in both the real world and the metaverse through their cartel network.
Long gone are the days of a singular kingpin and fighting over territory.

The Crypto Cartel collection is a unique set of digital collectibles, stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS.

CLASSIC COLLECTION:
7,000 UNIQUE CARTEL MEMBERS

The Crypto Cartel Classic Collection includes a distinctive variety of over 100 hand-drawn traits within the collection.
Limited to 7,000 pieces, each NFT is unique and acts as your key to the Cartel Metavilla.

The various character traits from this collection include:
  • Head: Hair and Headgear
  • Eyes: Eyes and Eye Gear
  • Ears: Ear Accessories
  • Jaw: Teeth, Expression and Facial Hair
  • Body: Complexion and Style
  • Dai: The Vice Hand
  • Bai: The Money Hand
  • Outfit: Clothing and Attire
  • Background: Pastel Base layer
  • Accessories: Wrist and Neck

GENESIS COLLECTION:
990 UNIQUE ANIMATED MEMBERS

The Crypto Cartel Genesis Collection includes more than 80 individually animated, uber special traits, over and above the 100+ hand-drawn traits from the Classic Collection.
Limited to 1,000 pieces, each unique NFT is highly rare and acts as your VIP key to the Cartel Metavilla.

Your Genesis Fella can have upto 3 animated traits, which include:
  • Animated Head: Hair and Headgear
  • Animated Eyes: Eyes and Eye Gear
  • Ears: Ear Accessories
  • Animated Jaw: Teeth, Expression and Facial Hair
  • Body: Complexion and Style
  • Animated Dai: The Vice Hand
  • Animated Bai: The Money Hand
  • Animated Outfit: Clothing and Attire
  • Animated Background: Retired Cartel-specific locations
  • Animated Accessories: Wrist and Neck
